RAMEN

Ingredient:

1. Korean instant ramyun

2. Chicken thighs 150-200gr

3. Green onions

4. 30 g garlic

5. 10 g butter

6. 1 egg

7. 1 tablespoon Korean red pepper powder

Prepare

    Cooking ramen

    1.The broth for ramen is cooked. The main types of broths used for cooking are: fish broth, dashi broth, meat broth. Roots, vegetables and various seasonings are added to almost all types of broths during cooking. According to the classification of broths, there are 4 main types of ramen: shio ramen, miso ramen, shoyu ramen and tonkotsu ramen.

    2.The noodles are boiled separately.

    3.The boiled noodles are placed in a bowl and filled with broth. The remaining ingredients are placed on top of the noodles: chashu, eggs, pickles and vegetables, nori, narutomaki or kamaboko. Ramen is eaten with chopsticks, and a porcelain or earthenware spoon of a special shape is also served to drink the broth.
